## (02) — The method — Four phases

## From problem to solution

**i. Diagnostic — free, 30 min**
Conversation about the operation and identification of the 2 or 3 most costly processes.
**What you get** — a written summary with the main areas for improvement identified and an honest recommendation: build, integrate, automate, or do nothing.

**ii. Solution design**
We map the process in detail with your team, design the solution and break it down into phases.
**What you get** — a proposal with a defined scope and timeline, and visual mock-ups of what will be built.

**iii. Building side by side**
Development in short cycles, with regular demos. Your team sees and uses the software before it's finished.
**What you get** — early access to the test version and adjustments based on real-world use.

**iv. Launch & guarantee**
Team training, going live, and a warranty period for fine-tuning included.
**What you get** — a user manual, code and documentation (yours to own), and the option of a maintenance and evolution retainer.

## (05) — Frequently asked questions

## The right questions<br>before hiring

**Who owns the code?**
You do. All code, documentation and access belong to the client at the end of the project.

**What if I need maintenance?**
We offer maintenance and evolution retainers. And since the code is yours and documented, any competent developer can continue the work.

**How much does it cost?**
It depends on the problem, but it's never a bottomless pit: case-by-case proposals, with the right to stop at the end of each phase.

**What if the developer disappears?**
The code is yours and documented so another developer can continue it. You're not locked into anyone — it's precisely the opposite of a closed box.

**Is my data safe?**
Hosted on European infrastructure, with controlled access and GDPR compliance. We explain exactly where your data is kept.
